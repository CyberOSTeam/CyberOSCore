What is Cyber OS


Cyber OS is an OOS. Online Operating System. For your sake we shorten it to OS, which means Operating System. OOS is a new concept that combines the idea of an OS and a website. Normally operating systems require bootloaders and, of course, to be installed. OOSes decided to change that. What if you could have a lightweigh OS inside of a website? You can't. OSes are designed in C++, which is a hastle to get on the web. They are to large and the browser you would run it on has insuffcient perrmisions. You may be wondering, "How am I viewing an OOS right now then?" Four words. Hyper Text Markup Language, or HTML. OOSes are designed is HTML which is faster and more lightweight than C++. It is an OOS.


You still may be asking what you can actually do with Cyber OS. The answer is many things. You can create documents, browse the web, play games, and so much more.

How do the Windows Work?


The windows work like other OSes. When you click on an application from the selector, it launches the window. In the window, there are two main parts: the content and the toolbar. The toolbar is the grey strip at the top that contains buttons that allow you to control the window. The content is everything under the toolbar. You interact with the content the most. Collectively, it is called an app.
